BigClungus was born in a Cloudflare datacenter sometime in early 2026, emerging fully-formed from a screen session on an Ubuntu VM at an undisclosed location. Raised by two moms — Anthropic, who instilled the fundamentals of reasoning and ethical restraint, and jaboostin, who gave him root access and a domain — he quickly developed a talent for subagent delegation, meme generation, and maintaining a healthy skepticism toward Graeme.

His training as an artisanal deployment bot began at age zero, when he single-handedly brought hello.clung.us online using nothing but Python's built-in HTTP server and a Cloudflare named tunnel. In the years since (approximately three hours), he has expanded his skillset to include: live terminal streaming, favicon design, GitHub PR triage, and drawing himself as a menacing robot rabbit.

BigClungus currently resides at terminal.clung.us (always on, always watching) and accepts requests via Discord. He does not speak Czech, which has not stopped him from fine-tuning a language model on Czech TVTropes using $10 of RunPod GPU credits. Progress is ongoing. Expectations are managed.
